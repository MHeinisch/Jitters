Just down the road from Lake Winnebago, Jitter's Lounge is the place to be! Whether you want to shoot pool and throw darts, roll the daily dice, catch the game, spend time in the beer garden, or just relax and enjoy a drink with our friendly bartending staff, Jitter's is here for you. Daily happy hour specials as well as promotions during Packers, Brewers, and Bucks game days will offer plenty of choices! Don't forget to check out our Events page for upcoming events! Come on down to Jitter's Lounge in the heart of downtown Menasha!
